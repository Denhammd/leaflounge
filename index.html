Care Tips

Watering 101

Most houseplants like moist (not soggy) soil. Stick your finger in an inch before watering.

Light Levels

Direct = harsh sun; Indirect = filtered or near a bright window; Low = far from windows.

Soil & Repotting

Use well-draining potting mix. Repot yearly or when roots start poking out.

Troubleshooting

Yellow leaves? Overwatering. Brown tips? Dry air or underwatering.
